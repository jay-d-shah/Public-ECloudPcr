Product Tour

See below for some of eCLOUDpcr's most innovative personal care report features.

Adaptive Form
When they do not apply to the current situation, needless entries disappear.

Common Problem

Time is often wasted when filling out digital patient records that ask for information irrelevant to a current emergency.

How We Solved It

eCLOUDpcr removes questions from patient forms in real time as they become irrelevant to that indivudual.

IntelliSearch
Why type out long entries? We know what you're thinking.

Common Problem

Many medical conditions and medications are very long and difficult to type out quickly with accuracy.

How We Solved It

Our Google-powered predictive text technology accurately fills in that information for the user.

TimeSync
For when timing really is everything.

Common Problem

In an emergency situation, it can be difficult to track and record time accurately.

How We Solved It

eCLOUDpcr adjusts the time stamps as you complete the form in real time based on available data.

Auto Calc
EMTs will never need a calculator again.

Common Problem

There are mathematical equations and conversions that must be commonly solved in the process of filing patient records.

How We Solved It

eCLOUDpcr performs calculations instantly as the user enters the data. This makes manual calculations a thing of the past.

Smart Location
Why type out long entries? eCLOUDpcr is always one step ahead.

Common Problem

Time is often wasted when filling out digital patient records that require redundant location data.

How We Solved It

Our Google-powered predictive text technology accurately fills in that information for the user.
